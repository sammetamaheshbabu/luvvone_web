About Us

Luvvone is a one-to-one, identity-locked communication app designed for couples who want a private space that feels calm, intimate, and secure.

Our mission

Build a dedicated space for two people—without social noise, public discovery, or distractions—while protecting your conversations with end-to-end encryption.

What makes Luvvone different

One-to-one by design

One verified person pairs with one approved partner. No public profiles or social graph.

End-to-end encrypted

Message content is designed to be encrypted on your device before it leaves it.

Built for intimacy

Features focus on shared moments and reliability, not engagement tricks.

Transparency

We explain how data is handled in our Privacy Policy and Data Policy. If you have questions, reach us anytime.
